About Us


Our team of skilled service engineers can supply expert support to offshore vessels anywhere in the world. Our focus is on the specialist marine technologies which the vessels servicing the offshore oil & gas industry rely upon. Our specialty is DP, Automation (WS, AMCS, IPC, PMS, PCS, FAS, etc.), also including navigation, communication, electrical/ PMS, engineering and marine engines, Thrusters, Gearbox, Propulsion, Hydraulics & Pneumatics’ system, Dry-docking supervisions & technical intellect on-board systems.

Our specialist engineers are experienced with all mandatory and industry required DP service, annual DP trials, FMEA verification, GAP analysis, operations manuals, and incident investigations, inspections. We also offer a remote supports for the DP program when access to vessels is restricted .

All engineers have the relevant offshore safety training and qualifications and work is performed according to class quality standards. SMD has a wide portfolio of products obtainable to outfit any customer requirement or market. Our products are as wide-ranging and qualitative as our specialist service. Each brand supports is wisely selected as a partner in supreme delivery and performance.


The team at SDM has satisfactory over 12 years of combined experience in the maritime, Oil & Gas industry. We are here to serve you anytime, anywhere
